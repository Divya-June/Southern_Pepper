Wonder why the volume of a drink is measured as Peg in India? It may sound surprising, but it is only in India that the standard quantity of liquor is referred to as Pegs, whereas globally drinks are measured as Shots. But do you know what exactly is a Peg and what’s the story behind this word? Well, if you too are inquisitive to know, read on as we share the lesser known story of ‘Peg’ and how this name became the measuring unit of your favourite drinks.

Juices

juices

Fruit juice is 100% pure juice made from the flesh of fresh fruit or from whole fruit, depending on the type used. It is not permitted to add sugars, sweeteners, preservatives, flavourings or colourings to fruit juice.Fresh juices are packed with soluble fiber, a type of fiber that supports good bacteria growth and digestive health. It has been shown to regulate blood sugar levels, lower cholesterol, and slow the transit of food through the digestive tract.
